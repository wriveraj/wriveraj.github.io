About
Background

In my current role and the last 8 years, I have been managing field inventory for a medical device division and overseeing quality control in high-volume distribution. For me, numbers were never abstract: a single miscount could mean a surgeon lacked the right implant.

That is the lens I bring to data. I am not just someone who learned SQL and Power BI in a bootcamp. I understand what the data is actually measuring, because I lived inside the operations that generated it.

Now I build analytical systems that surface the kind of problems that hide in plain sight: stockout risks, fulfillment failures, demand patterns that nobody caught until it was too late.
